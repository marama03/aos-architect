Can I White Label and Resell these AI tools to my clients?

White Label Demo

Yes! Whether you're on the $97, $297, or $497 GHL/HighLevel plan, you can resell AI solutions to your SaaS clients inside your GHL sub-accounts and charge whatever you want.

Most members make their investment back within the first few days to a week, everything after that is pure profit. Plus, the software runs tech-free, so you don't have to worry about complicated setups or maintenance!

Does this integrate with my GHL/HighLevel account?

Integration Screenshot

Yes! Our White Label AI integrates directly into your own GoHighLevel (GHL) agency account (not a sub-account under us). This gives you full ownership and control over your AI within your GHL ecosystem, allowing you to brand it, sell it, and scale it your way.

It works with all GHL plans: $97, $297, and $497, so you can start automating and growing your business regardless of your subscription level.
